ASIAN STICKY SPARERIBS

Asian Sticky Spareribs

Sexy asian spareribs that you can cook in under 90 minutes with the instant pot!

Ingredients

  • 3.5lb pork baby back ribs
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
    • For the dry rub:
  • 2tsp salt
  • 1tsp pepper
  • 2tsp togarashi
    • For the sauce:
  • 1/4cup sake
  • 1/4cup mirini
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1tbsp rice vinegar
  • 4tbsp brown sugar
  • 3tbsp honey
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 knob ginger

Steps

  1. in a small bowl mix together the dry rib ingredients
  2. remove membrane from back of ribs
  3. season ribs with dry rub
  4. set aside ribs for 30 min
  5. in small saucepan combine the sauce ingredients
  6. simmer on low heat for 30 min
  7. put ribs on top of the rack
  8. add 1 cup of water and 1/4cup apple cider vinegar
  9. select manual setting, high pressure for 25-30min
  10. natural release for 5 minutes, then quick release
  11. next we broil: put ribs in oven and brush with sauce and broil for about 3-4 min
